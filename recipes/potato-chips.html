Air Fryer Sweet Potato Chips

Use your air fryer to make these yummy sweet potato chips. After much experimenting I found that the best chips came from a mandoline that was set just a hair thicker than 1/16 inch (about 3/32 inch thick).

Ingredients:

  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1/4 tablespoon sea salt
  • 1/4 tea spoon black peppers
  • cooking spray

Steps:

  1. Slice potato. Soak in cold water. Dry with paper towels
  2. Return potato slices to dried bowl. Add oil, salt, and pepper; toss gently to coat.
  3. Preheat air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly coat air fryer basket with cooking spray.
  4. Cook until lightly golden and crispy, 12 to 16 minutes, turning and rearranging chips into an even layer every 4 minutes.
  5. Let chips cool about 5 minutes. Chips will continue to crisp as they cool.
