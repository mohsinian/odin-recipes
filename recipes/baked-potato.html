Microwave Baked Potato

When you're short on time or patience, waiting an hour or more for an oven-baked potato might seem like a tall order. That's why this recipe is so darn good â€“ you can have a perfectly microwaved baked potato in just a little more than 10 minutes!

It sounds too good to be true but, we promise, this recipe is just as good as reviewers say it is.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large sweet potato
  • Seasoning
  • Toppings
  • Butter
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps:

  1. Scrub your potato under running water, prick it with a fork, then place it on a microwave-safe plate.
  2. Microwave for about 5 minutes, flip it over, and microwave it for about 5 more minutes. Let the potato cool for just a few minutes, until it's safe to handle. Cut the potato lengthwise so that it opens and forms a natural bowl.
  3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, then mash the inside with a fork. Top with butter and Cheddar, then return to the microwave to melt the cheese. Finish it with sour cream and the toppings of your choice.
